Why use https on your website?!

2015-02-27 10:30 AM

Motivation

This is really popular story in last year. Maybe you have heard about cases of eavesdropping from NSA. This itself should attract attention. But, try to imagine that government don't have any attraction in your privacy. "HiHi."

What is motivation for use HTTPS? This web is static and hasn't any login form or other personal data.

Summary for strong configuration of HTTPS

  • No one can listen to your communication. For example on public wifi in your favorite coffee house or everywhere else.
  • It is impossible to change page content, ads etc. If you don't use SSL connection. Anyone can change their original ads. Or content of this article as heretical texts.

Solution

Before continuing. You need generate SSL certificate. You can generate your own SSL certificate Class II on website https://crt.simplia.cz. Price in time writing this article was really low (124 CZK / 5.12 USD / 4.51 EUR). More in this Souki's article.
